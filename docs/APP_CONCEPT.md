# App Concept (Concise)

## One-line concept
Walk Worthy helps Christians turn prayer into one concrete daily act of faith and short reflection, then tracks outcomes over time.

## Core loop
1. Pray (prompt)
2. Do (single action step)
3. Reflect (optional line)
4. Track (timeline of answered prayers)

## Product stance
- Private by default
- Offline-first and local-first
- No account required
- Simple enough to complete daily in under a minute

## Why this matters
The app closes the gap between spiritual intent and real-world follow-through without adding social pressure or complexity.
